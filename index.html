ABOUT M!RUS@M

MIRUS means something which is wonderful, strange, remarkable, amazing, surprising, extraordinary And This MIRUS word is decorated with Sanskrit word 'म्' that completes the name MIRUSम् . Mirusuम् is the official cultural fest of Department Of Computer Science And Information Technology (CSIT), Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. It is a vision by the Department of CSIT to provide a platform for its students to show their cultural skills and innovative ideas. Over the years Mirusum has proven itself as the landmark of entertainment and Unity, where all the students from different courses from the Department of CSIT come together as one to host the fest Mirusum. Mirusum 2019 will be celebrated on 16th November 2019. This year technical games are also introduced for the students, and team mirusum is looking bestow the event in a very wonderful way and every small contribution to make this event successful will be appreciated.
